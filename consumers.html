    Consumers

    Nostrum uses GenStage for event dispatching. A consumer is provided by the library in the form of Nostrum.Consumer. This provided process abstracts most of the "work" of defining consumers away from the user. With this consumer everything is handled including maintaining all of the internal caches.

    That said, it is still possible to define your own consumers, bypassing maintaining state within the lib itself.

    Implementing Custom Consumers

    To implement your own custom consumer you need to enable the option in your config. To do so, simply add custom_consumer: false to your config.

    To find the pids of the producers you need to subscribe to, you can use the method Nostrum.Util.producers/0.
